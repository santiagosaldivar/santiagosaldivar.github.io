# About Me

Hello! I am a Predoctoral Research Fellow in the Economics Department at the Ellison Institute of Technology, where my research focuses on health in the United Kingdom, with particular attention to heterogeneous trajectories of disease and wellbeing.
I graduated from Harvard College in 2024 with an A.B. in Applied Mathematics, focusing in Economics, and completed an undergraduate thesis on education economics. Prior to joining EIT, I was a predoctoral fellow at Harvard Business School and Massachusetts General Hospital (in collaboration with Harvard Medical School’s Department of Health Care Policy).
I plan to pursue a Ph.D. in Economics in the near future.
